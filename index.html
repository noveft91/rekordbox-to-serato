FAQ

Does it work with Rekordbox 7?▾
Yes. We support USB exports from Rekordbox 5, 6, and 7.
Will my CUE points transfer?▾
All hot cues with their colors transfer to Serato. Everything is preserved exactly as you set it up.
Do I need to re-analyze tracks?▾
No. BPM and key data transfer directly from your Rekordbox library.
What macOS versions work?▾
macOS 12 (Monterey) and later. Apple Silicon and Intel supported.
macOS says "Apple cannot verify this app" — how to open?▾
This is normal for apps distributed outside the App Store. To launch:

Option 1: Right-click (or Control-click) the app → click Open → click Open again in the dialog.

Option 2: Go to System Settings → Privacy & Security, scroll down and click "Open Anyway" next to the app name.

You only need to do this once — after that the app opens normally.
How do I enter my license key?▾
Open the app, go to Settings and paste the key from your email. It activates instantly.
Is there a Windows version?▾
Not yet. macOS only for now. Let us know if you want Windows support.
