Frequently Asked Questions

Does it work with Rekordbox 7? ▼
Yes, we support USB export from Rekordbox 5, 6, and 7. Just export your playlists to a USB drive and we'll handle the rest.
Will my CUE points transfer? ▼
Absolutely. All hot cues with their colors transfer to Serato. Everything is preserved exactly as you set it up.
Do I need to re-analyze tracks? ▼
No. We transfer BPM and KEY data directly from your Rekordbox library, so Serato gets the accurate info immediately.
What macOS versions are supported? ▼
macOS 12 (Monterey) and later. We keep the app lightweight and modern by focusing on recent OS versions.
Is there a Windows version? ▼
Not yet. We're macOS-focused for now, but we're exploring Windows support for the future. Let us know if you're interested!
